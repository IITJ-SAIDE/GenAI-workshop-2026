Accommodation at IIT Jodhpur

Limited accommodation may be available at the IIT Jodhpur Boys / Girls Hostels, subject to availability and institute norms.

Participants requiring accommodation at IIT Jodhpur are requested to fill out the Accommodation Request Form Accommodation is subject to availability and institute norms.
